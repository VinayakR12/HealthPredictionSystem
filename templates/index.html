Understand Your Health

Learn about common health conditions and how to prevent them.

Diabetes

  • Cause:High blood sugar due to insulin resistance.
  • Symptoms: Frequent urination, excessive thirst, slow healing wounds.
  • Risk Factors: Poor diet, sedentary lifestyle, obesity.
  • Prevention: Balanced meals, exercise, maintaining a healthy weight.

Heart Disease

  • Cause: Blocked arteries reducing oxygen supply.
  • Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath, dizziness.
  • Risk Factors: High cholesterol, smoking, stress.
  • Prevention: Exercise, healthy diet, stress management.

Parkinson’s Disease

  • Cause: Nerve cell damage affecting dopamine levels.
  • Symptoms: Tremors, muscle stiffness, slow movements.
  • Risk Factors: Aging, genetics, exposure to toxins.
  • Prevention: Regular exercise, antioxidants, staying mentally active.
